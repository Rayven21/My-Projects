About Us

Just over a year ago, we became fascinated by the idea of discovering the world through someone else eyes. What if you could see it through the eyes of a Cindy Sherman today or Robert Frank back then? This may sounds crazy but we wanted to build the finest web gallery in the history. While there are many ways to discover events and places, we perceived there is no better way to enjoy arts and galleries right now than a web based gallery. A bunch of pictures where it may be worth a thousand words, but a simple and convient gallery can take you someplace and show you the beauty of art.

Mission

The Block support the artists to continue creating aesthetically pleasing and phenomenal works that strives to serve and contribute to their profession and to the community.

Vision

The Block support the artists to continue creating aesthetically pleasing and phenomenal works that strives to serve and contribute to their profession and to the community.At the Block, our mission is to provide a chance for the aspiring and professional artists to feature their high-quality photography that preserves each and every memory.
